Advancing the shift to sustainable mobility

23-01-2022

We partnered with Rivian to create their visual identity and website. The goals of the project were the following:
  1. Create an imaginative and experiential validation of Rivian and its offerings
  2. Build excitement around the ambitions of Rivian and the debut of their Electric Adventure Vehicles
  3. Instill confidence in its investors and the media as to why Rivian is the automaker of the future.
  4. Drive pre-orders
view project

Core Technology

Harnessing WebGL, we created an interactive tour of Rivian’s technology to showcase how their all-electric vehicles are built and thoughtfully integrated to enable uncompromising exploration.

With interactions designed to meet visitors at their point of consideration, each decisive expression of the R1T and R1S can be easily explored – from the powertrain to air suspension, battery, and dynamic roll control.

Jiten Behl
Chief Strategy Officer at Rivian

Debuting Rivians first line of
EAVs - R1T and R1S

Aligning with Rivian’s team on all fronts, we launched product pages designed to encourage discovery, artfully unveiling the specifications of their first fleet of Electric Adventure Vehicles.

By thoughtfully integrating a pre-order system, Rivian was able to capture 1.8 B worth of pre-order deposits, converting fans to owners in a matter of clicks.
